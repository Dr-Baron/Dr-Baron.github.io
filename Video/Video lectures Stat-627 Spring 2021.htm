Professor Baron’s Statistical Machine Learning Lectures

 

Spring-2021

 

·       01-19-2021

Introduction, syllabus, course plan, examples. Main concepts of Statistical Machine Learning.

 

01-22-2021

Statistical machine learning concepts. Flexible and inflexible methods. Prediction mean-squared error and its three components. Degrees of freedom. R lab #2. 

 

01-26-2021 (HYBRID)

Review of mean-squared error of prediction. Tuning of SML algorithms. Practical example. Linear regression review: model, assumptions, the method of least squares, variance-covariance matrix, t-tests of significance.

 

01-29-2021

Regression review continued. T-tests and Partial F-tests. Extra sum of squares. Categorical predictors, dummy variables, and interactions.

 

02-02-2021

Regression: test of linearity; lack of fit and pure error. Classification: general approach; error classification rate; KNN algorithm.

 

02-05-2021 (HYBRID)

Classification methods. Classification rates. ROC curve, true positive and false positive rates. KNN algorithm.

 

02-09-2021

Logistic Regression. 

 

02-12-2021 (HYBRID)

Logistic Regression. Prediction. Threshold selection by (1) tuning, (2) loss function and risks. 

 

02-16-2021

LDA and QDA, linear discriminant analysis and quadratic discriminant analysis. 

 

02-19-2021 (HYBRID)

LDA, QDA. Computation of posterior probabilities. Evaluation, comparison, and selection of classification methods.

 

02-23-2021

Cross-validation and performance evaluation. Validation-set technique and leave-one-out cross-validation.

 

02-26-2021 (HYBRID)

Leave-one-out cross-validation and K-fold cross-validation. Preparation for jackknife: bias and unbiasedness.

 

03-02-2021

Jackknife, a tool for bias estimation and bias reduction. Based on these lecture notes.

 

03-05-2021  (HYBRID)

Jackknife and Bootstrap

 

03-09-2021

Bootstrap - review, applications, examples. Details are in these lecture notes and R labs.

 

03-12-2021 (HYBRID)

Jackknife and Bootstrap applications.

 

03-16-2021

High dimension and multicollinearity. Variance inflation factors.

 

03-19-2021 (HYBRID)

Dimension reduction methods. Variable selection: exhaustive search, sequential search. Introduction to ridge regression. Bootstrap confidence interval for the efficacy of the Pfizer COVID-19 vaccine.

 

03-23-2021

Shrinkage methods. Ridge regression.

 

03-26-2021 (HYBRID)

Ridge regression and LASSO. Theory and application. L2 and L1 norms. LASSO as a variable selection procedure. Bayesian nature of ridge regresion and LASSO.

 

03-30-21

Principal components as a method of dimension reduction and elimination of collinearity - definition, implementation, construction.

 

04-02-2021 (HYBRID)

Principal components. Principal components regression (PCR). Partial least squares (PLS).

 

04-06-2021

Splines.

 

04-09-2021 (HYBRID)

Trees

 

04-13-2021

Trees, bagging, and random forests.

 

04-16-2021 (HYBRID)

Random forest tuning. Support vector machines: maximal margin classifier.

 

04-20-2021

Support vector machines. Tuning - cost and kernel. Introduction to clustering. Hierarchical clustering and dendrogram.

 

04-23-2021 (HYBRID)

K-means clustering.
